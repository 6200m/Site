Forecast Channel

Check the local and international forecasts on your Wii.


The Forecast Channel works with RiiConnect24, and you can view the forecasts for several hundreds of cities. You can also view your local forecast on the Wii Menu. We use AccuWeather as the weather source, unlike Weathernews that Nintendo used.


Some games have compatibility with the Forecast Channel, and here's the stats for which ones are working with RiiConnect24:

  • Color Key

  • Green indicates the Forecast Channel compatibility of this game works with RiiConnect24.
  • Yellow indicates we're Not Yet Tested if the Forecast Channel compatibility of this game is working with RiiConnect24.
  • Red indicates the Forecast Channel compatibility of this game isn't working with RiiConnect24 right now.
