Wii Mail

Communicate with other Wiis again.


RiiConnect24 allows you to send mail to other Wiis again, as long as both Wiis have them connected to our service. Many games and apps rely on this feature to send data to other Wiis, so you can send Miis through the Mii Channel or send ghost data or friend rankings with Mario Kart Wii, for example.
